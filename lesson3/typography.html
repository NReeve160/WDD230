Header Typography: Abyssinica SIL serif

The place I have chosen is from a fictional place from a fictional podcast. It is based on in a fantasy setting, and for some reason I just think that headers that are fantasy connected should be serif fonts. I wanted to chose a serif that wasn't overly swirly or fancy though, since The Crick folk are a pretty simple folk. I was able to find this font that looks nice and has some very simple serifs, but still looks like it could easily be written.

Paragraph Typography: Font-Family: Ubuntu Sans Serif

I chose this one because I wanted a sans seriff font for the type of the page. I did this because The Crick folk are simple. I didn't think they would want anything to complicated for hte type. I stuck with Ubuntu because it is pretty well spaced between the words and comfortable beteween the letters. I think that this makes it much more readable in large chunks. Most of the informational websites is clumped together, so readability extremely important.
