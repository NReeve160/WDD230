Persona #1: The tourist

Many young people like to come to The Crick in order to explore and reconnect with nature. They will typically be between the ages of 22 to 35. They are attracted to The Crick because it is a affordable vacation destination that still has beautiful scenery in the Living Wood. Many of them are have only finished the minimum education requirements of their area, but some who are working on their continued education will come as well during time off of school. Those who do this will mostly be looking at the website through their Speaking Stones (Cell Phones). They are looking up places to visit, and historical information on The Crick.

Personal #1 Scenario #1

A young recently married couple has decided to spend their honeymoon in the Crick. They are well aware of the reputation for hospitality, but they were just curious and wanted to learn a little bit about the history of The Crick. The Chamber of Commerce is able to provide an overview of both the older history and recent history of The Crick.

Persona #1 Scenario #2

A young single woman is looking to see if it would be a good place for her to spend a weekend. She wants to make sure that it is a safe place for her and to know if it is the type of place that will let her enjoy a little bit of nature. When looking at The Crick Commerce site they can get an idea of the culture that exist in the Crick. Once she looks, she will be able to see that The Crick is especially known for its hospitality, though not necessarily for its safety.

Persona #1 Scenario #3

A group of potential adventurers were inspired by the stories of the Heroes of Bahumia. Since Moonshine Cybine is from the Crick they decided, they were going to begin as adventures at The Crick. This is an ideal place to begin since many of the people who are at the Crick are former high-level adventurers. Specifically, the Leader of the Crick, Maw Maw Gump, is well known for her adventuring skills. They decided to use the Chamber of Commerce site in order to find out a little more about the people and who they can reach out to about becoming adventurers.

Persona #2: The magical researcher

Magical researchers developed The Crick and its potential connection to the Fey Wild in recent years. As they have started to investigate this connection, they have referenced the chamber of commerce website more frequently when planning topography and magical study sites. They are interested in knowing what types of accommodations they can find, places that have a high magical density. They will often come on their own or with small teams of other researchers and assistants. They will do most of their planning on standard Personal Compute Stone (PC). Most of these researchers are High Elves from Gladeholm, between the ages of 400 to 500 years old.

Persona #2 Scenario #1

A magical researcher is looking into the connection between the Fey Wild and The Crick. They originally heard about it in the Legend of the Heroes of Bahumia. Persona #2 Scenario #2

The magical researcher from the previous scenario has finally received approval from the Gladeholm University to begin a expedition into the Living Wood. They have decided that they are going to set up a research base at The Crick. One of the first thing they looked into was how they are going to find living accommodations. This is actually a very simple problem, because as they will find out in chamber of commerce site, all property in The Crick is community property. So they are going to only need to choose a home and move in, and once they do they now have the house so long as at least one person from the research team stays there at all time.
