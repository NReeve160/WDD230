Miska Beverly Paw Paw II

Miska Beverly Paw Paw II is one of the only remaining dragons in Bahumia. A black dragon and child of Shadow Fang. She is having been moved to The Crick to ensure she has the best environment to grow into a loving dragon.

Grandma Tree

The Grandma Tree sits at the center of The Crick. This tree was created with the power of the first Mee Maw of The Crick. It is where all the town meetings are held. It is also where most of the Old Folks Circle lives.

The Crick Library

The Crick Library is one of the most unique libraries The books are all written on the spot, and quiet is highly discouraged. Included with the mostly outdoor design, you are unlikely to ever visit a library as unique as this one.
