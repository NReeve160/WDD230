Bountiful Foods

History

launched in 2016 by husband-wife team Dan and Kim Dill to share their passion for healthy eating. Both working professionals, committed to a healthy lifestyle and parents of two young girls, the Dills are not your typical café owners. After falling in love with the smoothie concept while on vacation, they quickly learned acai, a little-known fruit, boasts several healthy benefits and began experimenting with recipes to provide both savory and sweet options for friends and family. They saw the potential in California to provide a healthy alternative to your average breakfast or lunch spot, and as they say, the rest is history!
